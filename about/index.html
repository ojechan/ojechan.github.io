About

Who am I?

I don’t really know. Nobody really knows who they are.

Society in general chooses profession or academic position to define people. “I am X, and I do this. I study this. That is the entirety of who I am.”. I’m just gonna go with “I do not know. But you’re welcome to find out!”

What do I do?

Again I do not want to start with my profession. Let me start with my interests.

My interests vary over time, generally revolving around tech, reading and music. I read whatever I can get my hands on. Fiction, non-fiction, manga, magazines, doesn’t matter what the topic is. I just feel like I have to imbibe everything and anything.

I also consume a lot of movies, anime, and music. There’s just so much to encapsulate in words. Let me just say, nothing mainstream. Nothing commodified for viewer groups. I seek true passion. I seek to witness the will of the creator, eat the fruit of actual thought!

Consuming content is not my only interest in life. I love the process of creation. Right now my creations are in the form of words, and they are amateuristic. I hope that one day, my creations take the center stage. I hope that I will succeed in creating something which transcends my identity. That would surely be a life well lived.

Now for professional work. I currently work at Dell Technologies, in the analytics team supporting sales. My work typically involves automating sales reports primarily using Python. This involves a lot of browser automation, databases, automated emails and report generation. Previously I have worked in various ETL tools, creating data pipelines for almost all the major relational databases. I have also worked in creating real time B2B EDI interfaces.

I am also trained and certified in data science and analytics. My primary work experience is in Data Engineering, and I seek to explore more avenues in Data Engineering in my next roles.

I love having problems to solve, and the kick of engineering solutions keeps me going. I love learning new things, and I don’t want to stop learning ever!
